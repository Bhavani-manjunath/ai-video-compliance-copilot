## PROJECT TITLE
AI Video Compliance & Copyright Copilot

## PROBLEM STATEMENT
Creators using AI video tools often unknowingly violate copyright, licensing, and compliance rules, leading to takedowns, strikes, and legal risks.

## TARGET USERS
- Student content creators
- Educators
- Small media creators
- EdTech platforms

## FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
- Analyze video ideas and scripts
- Detect copyright and licensing risks
- Flag non-compliant assets
- Suggest safe alternatives
- Generate compliance reports

## NON-FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
- Easy to use
- Fast response time
- Explainable AI outputs
- Scalable for future use

## CONSTRAINTS
- No actual video rendering
- Prototype-level compliance checks
- Limited API usage
